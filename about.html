Versatile Immutability

We are a group of technopreneurs having worked in the blockchain field for more than four years. We see a great opportunity to reduce costs of current systems and bring a new service to market that has not existed before. Proving this is very well possible, we have created a blockchain-powered application to cater the market of anti-counterfeit (brand protection) / anti-theft (ownership tagging) & manufacturer big-data (visualization of interactions with items), serving the market with a solution – ready to implement.

We call this “LuxTag”.

We believe that we provide serious novel utility by leveraging on blockchain technology – and since no other application providers offer what we do, several key industry players will understand the importance of working with us.
